🇺🇸 US Tax Tricks — 18 Named Strategies

1Carried Interest LoopholeFund Managers

Cost: $1.5-3B/year
2Step-Up in Basis at DeathEveryone with Assets
Die with appreciated assets → heirs inherit at current value, not your purchase price. ALL lifetime gains wiped. Example: bought stock for $100k, worth $10M at death. Heirs sell → $0 capital gains tax on the $9.9M gain.
Cost: $40-60B/year
31031 ExchangeReal Estate
Sell investment property, buy another of equal/greater value within 180 days → defer ALL capital gains. Repeat infinitely. Never pay capital gains tax on real estate, ever.
Cost: $5-6B/year
4Opportunity ZonesReal Estate Developers
Roll capital gains into an OZ fund. Hold 10 years → $0 tax on ALL new appreciation from the OZ investment. 100% tax-free growth.
5QSBS Section 1202Startup Founders
Hold qualified small business stock (C-corp, <$50M assets) for 5+ years → exclude $10M or 10x basis from CGT. Stacking trick: family of 4 = $40M tax-free. Plus trusts = $10M each.
6QBI Deduction (Section 199A)Business Owners
Pass-through business owners (LLC, S-corp, sole prop) deduct 20% (now 23% post-OBBBA) of qualified business income. Up to $197,300 (single) / $394,600 (married) in 2025.
Cost: $50-60B/year
7SALT Cap PTE WorkaroundBusiness Owners in High-Tax States
SALT deduction capped at $10k. But pass-through entities can pay state taxes at entity level (not subject to cap). The $10k cap only hurts W-2 employees, not business owners.
Cost: $10-20B/year
8Mega Backdoor RothIf Your 401(k) Allows
Contribute after-tax $46,000 (2025) above regular $23,500 limit → convert to Roth. Total Roth capacity: $69,500/year. All future growth tax-free.
9Buy-Borrow-DieBillionaires
Buy assets → Borrow against them (tax-free loans) → Die → Heirs inherit at stepped-up basis (gains wiped). $0 income tax + $0 capital gains tax in entire lifetime.
10GRAT (Grantor Retained Annuity Trust)Ultra-Wealthy Families
Transfer assets to trust, receive annuity for set term. If assets outperform IRS rate, excess passes to heirs gift-tax-free. Walton family used this to transfer billions.
11Dynasty TrustsForever Families
Irrevocable trust in Delaware/Nevada/South Dakota (no rule against perpetuities). Lasts forever. Each generation benefits but assets never enter anyone's estate. Family vault, tax-free, forever.
12Donor-Advised FundsHigh-Income Earners
Contribute appreciated stock to DAF → immediate tax deduction for full value + $0 capital gains. $230B+ parked. No required distribution to charity. Ever.

🇭🇰 Hong Kong + 🇸🇬 Singapore

🇭🇰HK: No CGT, No VAT, No Estate DutyEveryone in HK
Hong Kong has no capital gains tax, no VAT/GST, no estate duty (abolished 2006), no dividend tax. If you buy stocks/property/art and sell for profit, the gain is NOT taxed. Wealth passes to heirs with no inheritance tax. One of the lowest tax burdens in the world.
🇭🇰HK: Two-Tier Profits Tax — 8.25% First $2MSmall Businesses
First HK$2M of profits at 8.25% (half rate). Above $2M at 16.5%. Example: $10M profit → effective rate ~14.85%. One of the lowest corporate rates in the developed world.
🇭🇰HK: Salaries Tax Capped at 15%All Employees
Progressive 2-17% OR flat 15% — you pay whichever is lower. For high earners, 15% flat is usually cheaper. The top rate in HK is lower than the basic rate in the UK (20%).
🇭🇰HK: Patent Box 5% (2025)IP Holders
Qualifying IP income at 5% — one of the lowest Patent Box rates in the world. Compare: UK Patent Box = 10%, Cyprus IP Box = 2.5%.
🇸🇬SG: Partial Tax Exemption — 8.9% EffectiveSmall Businesses
First S$10,000: 75% exempt. Next S$190,000: 50% exempt. S$200k profit → only S$105k taxable at 17% = S$17,850. Effective rate: 8.9%.
🇸🇬SG: Start-Up Exemption — 6.4% Year 1-3New Companies
First 3 years: 75% exempt on first S$100k, 50% on next S$100k. S$200k profit → S$75k taxable → S$12,750. Effective rate: 6.4%.
🇸🇬SG: VCC Structure for FundsFund Managers
Variable Capital Company: umbrella fund vehicle with tax-transparent sub-funds. Each sub-fund has separate legal personality. Used by hedge funds, PE, VC. The infrastructure of modern fund tax optimization.
🇸🇬SG: NOR Scheme — Time-Apportion Your IncomeQualifying Expats
Not Ordinarily Resident: only the portion of employment income corresponding to days in Singapore is taxable. Spend 60 days in SG, 305 abroad → only 60/365 taxed. For expats earning S$160k+.

HK + SG vs UK vs US

HK top income rate: 15%. UK top rate: 45% (60% trap). US top rate: 37%.
HK corporate: 16.5%. UK: 25%. US: 21%.
HK has no CGT, no VAT, no IHT. UK has all three. US has CGT + estate tax.
